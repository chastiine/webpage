The Rose Band

The Rose (더로즈) consists of 4 members: Hajoon, Woosung, Dojoon, and Jaehyung. The band debuted under J&Star Company on August 3rd, 2017 with their single ‘Sorry’. The Rose is currently an independent group following the members’ request of their contract’s termination due to mismanagement.

Why are they called The Rose?
“It’s a name to show music that coexisted with the beauty of flowers and the sharpness of thorns” – Woosung

UPDATE

The Rose Terminates Their Contract

The Rose, a four-member rock band, has had a rough journey with their former label J&Star Company.
Back in February of 2020, the members of the band accused the label of not providing any payment to
the members since their debut as well as demanding members stick to a rigorous promotional schedule.
After the fallout between the label and the members, member Woosung revealed in an interview that the issue had
been settled and their contract with J&Star Company had been terminated


The agency has announced that their exclusive contract with the boy band The Rose has officially been terminated after
the members filed a lawsuit against the agency for "manipulation of the artists".

"Beauty and the Beast" Release Date

On December 28, J&Star Company announced that the exclusive contract with The Rose will end with the
release of the single, ‘Beauty and the Beast’, and requested a lot of support for the members in their future activities.
Whether the group disbands or signs on with a new label will be determined once Dojoon, Hajoon, and Jaehyeong return from the military.
